1. (Codex) on the flight of birds

~7 mins — Violin & Cello

Score (PDF) · Recording

Commissioned by the Bowerbird Collective, this piece was written especially for the group and their “conservation storytelling” mission, “with the goal of strengthening emotional connections to nature through the arts.” Part of Life on Land's Edge, the piece evokes the precarity and grandeur of endangered migratory shorebirds as they traverse continents and cultures.

2. Trinomial Overshot 11

~3 mins — Fixed media / electronic

Listen on SoundCloud

Trinomial Overshot 11 seeks to forge a new and distinct relationship to “the grid” and to periodicity itself. Inspired by the unusual weaving patterns devised by American weaver Ada Dietz, I wrote an algorithm combining OpenMusic and Common Lisp that generates and allows me to audition stochastic, hierarchical patterns of irregular pulse duration. This piece is built entirely around five such rhythms, each of eleven pulses, colliding with additional aleatoric elements. The title, Trinomial Overshot 11, refers to Ada Dietz’s own beautiful tapestry of the same name.
